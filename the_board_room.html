The Board Room

A warm, social space made for sitting down, playing games, and spending time together. The Board Room is designed for small groups, families, and friends looking for an easy, relaxed way to unwind.

The Board Room is centered around shared play, with a layout built for gathering around the table and enjoying familiar games.

You can sit down and play familiar games like chess, checkers, Scrabble, Monopoly, Uno, Apples to Apples, or just open a deck of cards and start a quick game.

The room is focused on familiar games and easy, group play.

When you're not playing, the space stays comfortable and low–key — soft lighting, chairs you can settle into, and enough space that it never feels crowded or loud.

It is a place to sit down, play a few rounds, and enjoy the time together.

  • Wide selection of classic board games and card games
  • Large shared tables designed for group play
  • Comfortable seating for longer games
  • Game shelves within easy reach for quick selection
  • Soft ambient lighting with a warm tone
  • Extra decks of cards and simple grab–and–play games
  • Charging access for phones and personal devices
  • Quiet, relaxed atmosphere for conversation and play

A space built for playing together.
